Features

More features than any other player on macOS

Plays Everything

Every format, every codec, no exceptions. MKV, MP4, AVI, FLAC, and hundreds more, all hardware-accelerated via VideoToolbox. Files that choke other players just work.

Seekbar Thumbnails

Hover over the seekbar to see thumbnail previews of any moment in the video. Jump to exactly the right scene without guessing.

Music Mode

Not just a video player. Dedicated music experience with album art, audio visualizer, gapless playback, ReplayGain, and a compact mini player that floats above everything.

Synced Lyrics

Karaoke-style, time-synced lyrics right in music mode. Fetched automatically from LRClib or read from local .lrc files, with the current line highlighted and click-to-jump on any line.

Stream Anything

Paste any URL. YouTube, Twitch, and thousands more. Quality selection, SponsorBlock, cookie auth, and subtitle fetching. No browser needed.

HDR & EDR

Extended Dynamic Range output for HDR content on supported displays. Accurate tone mapping presets for SDR screens.

Obsessively Customizable

200+ settings, 8 themes, searchable preferences, and community presets for anime, cinema, home theater, and more. No other player on macOS comes close.

Real-Time GLSL Shaders

The only macOS player with a built-in shader gallery. Anime4K upscaling, adaptive sharpening, CAS. Browse, stack, and toggle shaders in real time.

Album Art & Video Previews in Finder

Every song and video gets real album art or a video frame as its Finder icon, including formats macOS normally shows as a generic music note or filmstrip. Covers MP3, FLAC, M4A, MKV, WebM, Opus, OGG, MP4, MOV, WAV, DSD, and more, with Quick Look preview support to match.

Best-in-Class Subtitles

Full ASS/SSA styling, OpenSubtitles search, dual subtitle tracks, and frame-precise timing. Every format supported, perfectly rendered.

Playlists & Library

Drag-and-drop playlist with thumbnails, media library with folder scanning, and session persistence across launches.

Picture-in-Picture

Floating mini player stays on top while you work. Resize freely, control playback, and snap it to any corner of your screen.

Unlimited Power

Full mpv.conf passthrough. Set any of mpv's hundreds of options directly. Zero artificial limits. If mpv can do it, Corvus can do it.

Audio Visualizer

Real-time peak and RMS level meters per channel. Three visualization styles that respond to your music with smooth animations.

Chapter Navigation

Browse and jump between chapters with a visual chapter list. Embedded MKV chapters, YouTube chapters, and SponsorBlock segments, all in one place.

Smart Shortcuts

Fully customizable keyboard shortcuts and trackpad gestures. Pinch to zoom, swipe to seek, two-finger tap to pause, or remap everything to your liking.

36 Languages

Fully localized in 36 languages including Arabic, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Thai, and more. Searchable settings work in every language. RTL support for Arabic and Hebrew.

Audiophile Equalizer

A dedicated Equalizer tab with a 10-band parametric EQ, adjustable per-band width, and saveable presets. Device Equalization brings AutoEQ corrections for 6,000+ headphones and earphones, matched to your output automatically, plus crossfeed, loudness, balance, and per-device EQ memory.

Night Mode & Smart Playback

Night mode evens out loud and quiet passages so late-night dialogue stays clear. Queue what plays next with Up Next without reordering your playlist, pinch to zoom and pan into the video, and jump back into streams instantly with on-disk caching.

Built for macOS

A menu bar mini player, Shortcuts and Spotlight support, and a Focus filter for hands-off control. Find subtitles from SubDL as well as OpenSubtitles, with one-tap styling presets.

Why Corvus

Why nothing else compares

mpv Under the Hood

Powered by the same engine trusted by millions worldwide. But unlike raw mpv, Corvus wraps it in a beautiful native interface without sacrificing a single option.

Truly Native macOS

Built from the ground up with SwiftUI and AppKit, not a cross-platform port. Liquid Glass on macOS Tahoe, Quick Look extensions, Dock menus, and system integration that feels like it came from Apple.

Zero Compromise Privacy

No accounts. No telemetry. No tracking. No cloud. Your media never leaves your Mac. Credentials live in the system Keychain. We don't even have a server to spy on you.

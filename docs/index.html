Corvus
Player

A powerful, customizable media player for macOS. Built on mpv with hardware-accelerated playback, GLSL shaders, music mode, and streaming support.

Download for macOS macOS 14.0+ · Apple Silicon & Intel
Features

Everything you need in a media player

Universal Playback

Play virtually any format: MKV, MP4, AVI, FLAC, and hundreds more. Hardware-accelerated decoding via VideoToolbox keeps your Mac cool.

GLSL Shaders

Built-in shader gallery with Anime4K upscaling, adaptive sharpening, and CAS. Browse, toggle, and combine shaders with one click.

Music Mode

Dedicated music experience with album art, audio visualizer, gapless playback, ReplayGain, and a compact mini player that floats above your work.

Streaming

Play URLs from YouTube, Twitch, and thousands of sites via yt-dlp. Quality selection, SponsorBlock, and subtitle fetching built in.

HDR & EDR

Extended Dynamic Range output for HDR content on supported displays. Accurate tone mapping presets for SDR screens.

Deeply Customizable

8 themes, 200+ settings with searchable preferences, community presets for anime, cinema, and more. Make it yours.

Subtitles

Full ASS/SSA styling, OpenSubtitles integration, secondary subtitle tracks, and precise timing controls. Every subtitle format supported.

Playlists & Library

Drag-and-drop playlist with thumbnails, media library with folder scanning, and session persistence across launches.

28 Languages

Fully localized in 28 languages including Arabic, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Thai, and more. Searchable settings work in every language.

Why Corvus

Built for power users

mpv Under the Hood

The same battle-tested engine trusted by millions. Every mpv option is accessible, and raw config passthrough lets you fine-tune anything.

Native macOS Experience

Built with SwiftUI and AppKit. Liquid Glass on macOS Tahoe, native keyboard shortcuts, Quick Look extensions, and Dock menu integration.

Privacy First

No accounts, no telemetry, no tracking. Your media stays on your Mac. Credentials are stored in the system Keychain.
